About me

Hi! It’s nice to see you here. My name is Valeriia, and I have just started my career path. I try to combine web development, graphic design, and digital marketing in my work, and I would love to tell you some more information about my skills. So buckle up!

Web Development

I enjoy creating thoughtful and yet engaging web applications. I am very passionate about my work and always open to learning new frameworks and strive for progression. So far I’ve used HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, SQL, Gulp, and Webpack to create my projects.

Graphic Design

Digital Marketing
